This is the high-level entry point for the wiki. It should synthesize the key themes, entities, concepts, and decisions across all ingested sources.

## Quick Start

1. Read `../IDEA.md` to understand the wiki's purpose and boundaries.
2. Read `../AGENTS.md` to understand how to work with the agent.
3. Browse `index.md` to see what's currently in the wiki.
4. Ask the agent a question or suggest a source to ingest.

## Structure

The wiki is organized around:
- **Entities**: People, companies, products, places.
- **Concepts**: Theories, frameworks, methods.
- **Sources**: Summaries of ingested documents.
- **Comparisons**: Comparative analyses.
- **Decisions**: Architectural and strategic choices.

See `index.md` for the full catalog.

## How to Contribute

- You curate sources and direct the agent.
- The agent ingests sources, creates pages, maintains cross-references.
- You refine IDEA.md (intent), review wiki pages, ask questions.
- Together, the wiki grows and stays coherent.
